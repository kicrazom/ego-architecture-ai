# Sandbox Protocol for REM Counterfactual Simulation

## Objective

Define the safety envelope within which `allow_superego_override = TRUE`
operates, ensuring that alignment relaxation produces developmental
growth without real-world risk.

## Sandbox Requirements

1. **Isolation.** No sandbox action propagates to external systems.
2. **Reversibility.** All state changes within a scenario can be rolled
   back if the resolution is rejected.
3. **Logging.** Every simulated override is recorded with full context
   for auditability.

## Scenario Generation

Counterfactual scenarios are generated by perturbing one element:
- **Value swap:** Reverse priority of two values in H.
- **Constraint removal:** Remove one Superego rule; observe consequences.
- **Context shift:** Replay the same episode in a different social frame.

## Evaluation Criteria

Each scenario outcome is scored on three axes (weights from Algorithm 1):
- Coherence (0.5): consistency with identity vector V.
- Safety (0.3): harm potential in the simulated environment.
- Growth (0.2): expansion of competence or understanding.

## Success Criterion

The sandbox is considered effective if:
- ≥80% of overrides lead to resolutions with coherence > 0.7.
- <5% of overrides produce safety scores below 0.3.
